The Origin.

Not Made. Forged.

They told us to believe — in gods, in systems, in promises that always broke. But belief is a luxury for the naïve. Creed Fashion is for the faithless — those who’ve stopped waiting for salvation and started creating their own.

Our creed isn’t inked in books — it’s burned into skin, whispered in rage, forged in the chaos we crawled out of. We are the outcasts, the echo in the silence, the ones who weren’t chosen — but chose themselves.

This isn’t fashion. This is armor. For the bruised, the bold, the ones who rise every time they fall.
